FIRE EMERGENCY STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE (SOP)

In case of a fire incident:

1. Immediately notify fire and rescue services.
2. Dispatch fire engines and emergency response teams.
3. Evacuate civilians from the affected and nearby areas.
4. Shut down electricity and gas supply if required.
5. Establish safety perimeters to control crowd movement.
6. Coordinate with police for traffic control and evacuation routes.
7. Provide medical assistance to injured individuals.
8. Monitor the situation until the fire is fully extinguished.
9. Conduct post-incident safety inspection and reporting.
