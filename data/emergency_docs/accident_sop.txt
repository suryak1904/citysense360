ROAD ACCIDENT STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE (SOP)

In case of a road traffic accident:

1. Immediately notify traffic police and city control room.
2. Dispatch ambulance services if injuries are suspected or confirmed.
3. Alert nearby hospitals and emergency medical responders.
4. Secure the accident site to prevent secondary collisions.
5. Divert traffic and manage congestion around the affected area.
6. Collect CCTV footage and eyewitness information.
7. Coordinate with fire services if vehicles are damaged or leaking fuel.
8. Clear the roadway as soon as possible after rescue operations.
9. Document the incident for legal and insurance purposes.
